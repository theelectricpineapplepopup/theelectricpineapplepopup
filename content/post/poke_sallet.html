“… probably the best known and most widely used wild vegetable in America.”

  • Euell Gibbons (needs verification)

Characteristics, Ecology, History

  • Phytolacca americana
  • Phytolaccin
  • Native to North America (also possibly found in Africa)
  • Appalachian staple
  • Associated with poor, rural south (e.g. “Poke Salad Annie”)
  • Exported to Europe as an ornamental
  • Used by the Algonquin for dye
  • Civil War Era Letters possibly written in Pokeberry ink

Pigeon Berry

  • Eaten in large quantities by passenger pigeons
